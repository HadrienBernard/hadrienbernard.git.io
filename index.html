Koreazur festival

In March 2024, I had the honour to be part of the cultural Korean festival "Koreazur" in Aix en Provence. This was one of the best professional and human experience of my career so far. Indeed, as a team of 11 students, we managed to organize a whole festival with several activities through a fortnight. I've been involved in many projects such as Norigae crafting, traditional korean games, projection of korean films. I also had the chance to present my own lecture entitled "North Korean ressources and time projections" in front of students and teachers as well as the big closing quiz. Besides being a staff member, I was the community manager for the instagram page of the festival to enhance and cover the communication about the ongoing activity with pictures and videos.
